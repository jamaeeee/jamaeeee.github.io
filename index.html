Penne Alla Vodka

Alright, here’s the scoop, capisce? Penne alla Vodka is this real classy dish, ya know? Picture it: penne pasta smothered in a rich tomato sauce with a lil' heavy cream to make it smooth. Then ya throw in a splash of vodka—don’t worry, it ain’t about gettin’ tipsy, it just brings the flavors together real nice. Top it off with some Parmesan cheese, maybe a bit of pancetta if you’re feelin' fancy. It's creamy, it's tangy, and it's the kinda meal that'll make ya say, "Fuhgeddaboudit!"


Ingredients

  • Olive Oil
  • Penne Pasta
  • Tomato sauce
  • Vodka
  • Basil

Steps

  1. Boil the penne: Get a pot of salted water goin’. Cook it al dente. We ain’t overcooking nothin' here.
  2. Sauté garlic and pancetta: Heat some oil, throw in the garlic and pancetta. Let 'em sizzle.
  3. Add vodka: Pour in a good splash of vodka—don’t set the place on fire!
  4. Tomato sauce & cream: Toss in the tomato sauce, then add cream to smooth it out.
  5. Mix it all up: Drain the pasta, toss it in the sauce, and hit it with some Parmesan. Done!
